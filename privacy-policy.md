# Privacy Policy  
**Glass Creations Hub LLC**  
Effective Date: 2/1/2026

Glass Creations Hub LLC ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your information.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

Our application may access and store business-related data through authorized integrations with QuickBooks Online and QuickBooks Time (formerly TSheets), including:

- Employee names and employee IDs  
- Timesheet records (clock-in and clock-out times)  
- Notes entered on timesheets  
- Paid and unpaid break information  
- Invoice-related data necessary for customer billing  

We only access data that is required for internal business operations.

---

## 2. How We Use the Information

We collect and store personal and financial data necessary to:

- Track employee timesheets  
- Calculate paid and unpaid work hours  
- Monitor payroll reporting  
- Generate invoices for customers  
- Maintain internal financial and operational records  

This application is used strictly for internal business purposes by Glass Creations Hub LLC.

---

## 3. Data Sharing

We do **not** sell, rent, or share your data with any third parties.

Data accessed through QuickBooks APIs is used solely within our internal systems and secure business tools (such as Airtable or internal databases).

---

## 4. Data Security

We use industry-standard security practices to protect your information, including:

- Secure HTTPS connections  
- OAuth authentication via Intuit  
- Restricted internal access to business systems  
- Secure cloud-based storage platforms  

---

## 5. Data Retention

We retain employee and financial data only as long as necessary for payroll, accounting, and regulatory compliance purposes.
